01 / Background & Working Style

A product designer who thinks in systems

I'm a Senior AI Product Designer specializing in SaaS platforms, analytics dashboards, AI-powered workflows, and scalable design systems. I've contributed to 10+ shipped products across AI, Analytics, PropTech, LegalTech, and EdTech.

I've been a Product Designer at Duskbyte since 2021, working from Punjab, India. I'm specialized in AI UX patterns, dashboard design, onboarding flows, and design systems — built in Figma, with variables, and handed off through Webflow and Framer.

My work has supported products used by 40,000+ end users and helped automate workflows by up to 75%. I focus on interfaces that improve adoption, conversion, and business outcomes — not just ones that look good in a file.

02 / How I Think and Build
A.01 // Systems

Design the logic before the layout

States, permissions, and edge cases get worked out before a single pixel is styled. A product that only works on the happy path isn't finished — it's a demo.

A.02 // Outcomes

Adoption is a design metric

Retention, automation, and conversion are downstream of design decisions. I track what happens after launch — not just whether the ship date was hit.
